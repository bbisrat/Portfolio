About Me

Hi, I'm Betel Bisrat, a Computer Science student at Carleton University, with a stream in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, and a minor in Psychology. My academic journey is complemented by a passion for personal projects where I continuously apply and expand my knowledge in the field.

I've developed proficiency in various programming languages and technologies, including Python, Java, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, C and database management with such as SQL. My development environment is diverse, covering both Linux and Windows platforms, and I'm proficient in using tools like Visual Studio Code and IntelliJ, along with version control systems such as GitHub/Git.

Additionally, I have a keen interest in expanding my knowledge, especially in areas that spark my curiosity, through YouTube videos and earning certifications.

As I continue to grow and learn in this dynamic field, I am eager to apply my skills and knowledge in practical settings. I'm committed to contributing innovative solutions to complex challenges and solving problems in the field of software engineering, AI, ML, Web developed.
